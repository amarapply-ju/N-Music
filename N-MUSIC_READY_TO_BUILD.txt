🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

Build fails with "ANDROID_HOME not set":
  export ANDROID_HOME=$HOME/Android

Build fails with "JAVA_HOME not set":
  export JAVA_HOME=/usr/lib/jvm/java-17-openjdk-amd64

App crashes on launch:
  Check logcat: adb logcat | grep N-music
  Review app/build.gradle.kts for any errors

Font not appearing on titles:
  Clear app data: adb shell pm clear moe.koiverse.archivetune
  Restart app

Theme selector not working:
  Check Settings → Appearance
  Ensure DarkMode enum is properly implemented
