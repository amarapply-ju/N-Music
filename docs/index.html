Ad-Free Playback

Enjoy uninterrupted music with background listening support. Your listening, your way.

🔒

Privacy First

No tracking, no invasive permissions. Your music history stays yours alone.

✨

Beautiful Design

Nothing OS inspired aesthetic with glassmorphic components and dot-matrix typography.

🎚️

Full Control

Equalizer, crossfade, playback speed, and deep customization for power users.

📝

Live Lyrics

Synced lyrics with translation and romanization support for global artists.

🎨

Dynamic Colors

Album art-powered color themes and Material 3 design with full customization.
