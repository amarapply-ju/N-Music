[ 04 / FAQ ]

Questions?

The short answers. The longer ones live on GitHub.

Is N-Music really free?

Yes. Free as in beer and free as in freedom. MIT licensed. No paywalls, no premium tier, no nonsense.

How is it different from Spotify?

No subscription, no ads, no tracking, no account required. It's a clean Nothing-styled wrapper around the YouTube Music catalogue.

Where does the music come from?

YouTube Music's public catalogue. N-Music is a player; it does not host or redistribute audio.

iOS support?

Not yet. Android-first. iOS is on the roadmap if the community wants to help.

How can I contribute?

Star the repo, file issues, send PRs. Designers and devs welcome. Every dot counts.
